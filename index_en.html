1D6 XVM MODPACK is a minimalist preconfigured pack containing the most "useful" mods. It is well for players who want to install only the essentials. An automatic installer takes care of everything and a modpack version checker will alert you in game when new version is available. It includes the following features and mods:

XVM Configuration
  • Displays WN8 stats on 4 digits
  • Displays players nicks on the minimap
  • Displays ping servers on the login and garage screens
  • Autologin to the last server used
  • Multi-lines Carousel with extra informations (number of battles, win rate, performance, mastery mark, marks of excellence)
  • Displays Hitlog on 15 lines
  • Displays Damagelog (received damage)
  • Displays efficiency statistics for individual missions (Damage dealt, received, blocked, assist, squad,...)
  • Displays repair timer of damaged module
  • Audio alerts : 6th sense (Metal Gear Solid), Fire, Ammobay
  • Ratings Colors scale on 10 levels by wotlabs
  • Displays Full screen of the minimap by pressing L-CONTROL key
  • Alternative Player Panel & Minimap by pressing L-ALT key
  • Custom Markers (display WN8, national flag, winrate)
  • Displays 6th sense icon for 9 seconds (max spotted time)
Included Mods
  • ModPack Version Checker by webium
Optional Mods
  • XVM eXtended Visualization Mod
  • Panteror's Contour Icon Mod
  • Panteror's Gold Premium Tank & Special Icons
  • J1mB0,Harpoon,Taipan,Hardscope,Meltymap,Warrior Crosshairs
  • No Dynamic (Disable gun shake effect after shot with dynamic camera)
  • Zoom In/Out (Extends max sniper zoom & zoom out range)
  • Battle Assistant by reven86 (Second arty PoV by pressing G or middle-mouse button)
  • Session Statistics by P-MOD (own custom config)
  • Received Damage Announcer by Elkano (Displays in chat box who damaged you with extra infos : gold, teamdamage)
  • TotalHP by P-MOD (Live HP Pool for both teams)
  • battleEfficiency (In Game WN8 live calculation)
  • WN8_Chance (Displays winning percentage & teams WN8)
  • Safe Shot (Blocks shooting at wrecks and allies)
